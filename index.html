So it was a really tight knitt community. hated when old people left and hated even more when new people moved in. I kinda knew what I was always meant to do growing up, to be a vetrinarian. Then came highschool, I wasn't really the smartest with math and science, known as the two main things I needed to be for a vetrinarian. So I explored other options. and fell in love with Communications Technology. I had a teacher who really helped me find out what worked perfectly for me. this being broadcasting, and Graphic Design.
